My Top 5 Favorite Animated Series

  1. Adventure Time

    I believe Adventure Time has some of the most creative world building
    and even though things do get serious, it maintains a soft and playful
    tone throughout the series.

    You can stream this show on Max!

  2. Amphibia

    Amphibia was a pleasant surprise when watching the first time.
    It goes from a silly concept of a girl in a frog world to having a crazy
    conflict between her 2 best friends while facing a war between amphibians.
    The art style seems simple but can get so mesmerizing when animated!

    You can stream this show on Disney Plus!

  3. Steven Universe

    This show can be an emotional rollercoaster at times but has such a pretty
    format in storytelling and design. There is foreshadowing dating back to the
    first episodes and I always stumble upon a new one every rewatch. It has a
    bunch of songs that continue to stick with me and a beautiful aesthetic.

    You can stream this show on Max!

  4. Gravity Falls

    Gravity Falls is a mystery based show where you can find foreshadowing
    and secret codes in almost every episode. It never fails to keep you guessing
    and wanting for more. It also has some of the funniest jokes that still continue
    to make me laugh!

    You can stream this show on Disney Plus!

  5. The Owl House

    This show had a strong beginning and a very cool concept about a human girl wanting to
    be a witch. The art style is my favorite out of these and the characters
    progress in very different ways than where they started. Unfortunately, this show
    had to face a very abrupt cut in it's final season and didn't end properly.

    You can stream this show on Disney Plus!
